Olivia Rodrigo's Debut Album: SOUR

Olivia Rodrigo's debut album broke chart records.
  On April 13, Olivia announced on social media her first album title "SOUR", 
  that would be released May 21st, 2021. She also posted the album's tracklist 
  and cover art. (as shown below)

The Hit Ballad "Drivers License"

Realesed early January. The song generated 100 million streams & debuted at No. 1 on the Billboard Hot 100. Now as of November of 2022 it has over a billion streams, along with 3 other songs from the SOUR album.
